Our Story

How we convert waste into climate‑positive, circular economy value

From waste challenge to circular economy solution

Neofy was founded to address a systemic gap: the disconnect between Africa’s escalating organic waste and its untapped potential to drive regenerative agriculture, youth employment, and climate resilience.

Our journey began with Incipe, a research‑driven platform that introduced us to the transformative potential of Black Soldier Fly (BSF) bioconversion. Through their technical insights and ongoing support, we gained the foundational knowledge to design modular systems that convert organic waste into high‑value agricultural inputs. Incipe’s research continues to inform our operational strategy, system design, and impact metrics.

What started as a prototype in Dar es Salaam has evolved into a scalable, fundable platform architecture—deploying decentralized bioconversion hubs that embed circular economy principles into local ecosystems. Neofy is not a concept. It’s a working model. We build, we operate, and we scale.

About Us

Our units transform organic waste into protein‑rich larvae and organic fertilizer— feeding soils, reducing emissions, and creating inclusive economic opportunity.

Mission

To convert organic waste into regenerative value—building scalable, locally operable systems that drive circular economies, empower communities, and strengthen climate resilience across Africa.

Vision

A continent where waste is no longer a burden but a resource—where decentralized bioconversion hubs fuel agricultural productivity, youth employment, and systemic climate solutions.

Our Solution

Three product lines enable waste‑to‑value operations end‑to‑end

Modular BSF Units

  • Modular systems with environmental control
  • 1–50 tons/day processing configurations
  • Rapid commissioning and operator training

Protein Feed

  • Defatted BSF meal for poultry and aquaculture
  • Stable amino acid profile, low FCR
  • Replaces fishmeal/soy in rations

Organic Fertilizer (Frass)

  • Soil‑building, microbe‑rich amendment
  • Improves water retention and yields
  • Complement to existing nutrient plans
